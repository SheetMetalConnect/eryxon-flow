v0.6 is the stable release you install from the browser. On Windows or macOS, open Eryxon Flow in
Chrome or Edge, install it, and it runs in its own window like any desktop app — no store, no
installer, no rebuild when your config changes.

Alongside the PWA, a touch-first operator preview brings the work queue, scanner, and terminal
flows to phones and tablets. The native iOS and Android apps are still in development — follow them
on the [public roadmap](/roadmap/). They are not released yet, so do not treat them as shipped.
