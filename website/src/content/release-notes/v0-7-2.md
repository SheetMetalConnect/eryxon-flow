Two faults from the v0.7.1 rollout showed up only against real, high-volume floors: the operator terminal could go blank when a batch lookup failed, and admins were forced through the operator badge screen on mobile. Both are fixed, and the off-by-default location module no longer touches the terminal when it is disabled.
