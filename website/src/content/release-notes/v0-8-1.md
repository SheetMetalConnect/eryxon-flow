A quick follow-up to v0.8.0. The operator terminal labelled the Bullet Card with a generic "Rush" badge — but there is no separate rush concept in Eryxon Flow: the Bullet Card is the priority signal. The chip now reads **Bullet**, matching the card's name everywhere else. The card itself is unchanged.

We also cleared two stale articles that had been duplicated into the documentation sidebar; articles live on the blog, and the old links redirect there.
