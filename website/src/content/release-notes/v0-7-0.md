v0.7 closes the gap between what happens on the floor and what the system knows about it. A part
carries its current cell and its next one. An issue holds that same context, so a problem points
back to where it started. The work queue reads in manufacturing terms (material, thickness,
tooling, batches) instead of a flat task list. And the operator terminal knows whether work is
ready for setup or ready for production, on a tablet that a whole cell can share.
