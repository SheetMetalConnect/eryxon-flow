Most teams start free and explore on their own. A **managed rollout** is for shops that want help getting Eryxon Flow into daily production: deployment, ERP integration, and a sensible rollout order across cells and people.

A conversation, not a signup. Tell us about your shop and we'll work out the right path together.

## Which path fits you?

### Hosted trial

Open the product now and see how it feels. No install, no setup.

- Best for: a quick first look and hands-on exploration.
- Go to [app.eryxon.eu](https://app.eryxon.eu).

### Managed rollout

Want help putting Eryxon Flow into production? We can help with deployment, connecting your ERP via the REST API and webhooks, and sequencing the rollout so the shop floor adopts it without disruption.

- Best for: shops that want a guided deployment and ERP integration.
- Use the inquiry button above to start the conversation.

### Self-hosted evaluation

Comparing deployment models or evaluating on your own infrastructure? Eryxon Flow is source-available under BSL 1.1 and free for internal use when self-hosted.

- Best for: technical evaluators who want to run it themselves.
- Start with the [Deployment Guide](/guides/deployment/) and [Self-Hosting Guide](/guides/self-hosting/).

## What to expect

When you reach out about a managed rollout, it helps to share:

- what you make and roughly how many active jobs you run,
- which ERP or planning system you'd want to connect,
- whether you're leaning toward hosted or self-hosted,
- your rough timeline.

We'll reply by email to set up a conversation.
