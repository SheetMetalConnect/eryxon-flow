What it is

An MES built for high-mix, low-volume job shops.

01 — Built for fabricators

Sheet metal, machining, custom fabrication.

Designed around routings through cutting, bending, welding, assembly, finishing — not generic factory work.

02 — Tablet-first

Real touch-friendly screens.

56-pixel touch targets. Glanceable from a metre. The operator doesn't read — they look.

03 — Source-available

BSL 1.1 — yours to self-host.

Free to use for your manufacturing business. Run on Docker, on your own infra, with your own database.

04 — Plays nice

REST, MQTT, planning adapters.

30+ REST endpoints. Adapters for FrePPLe and Odoo MRP. MQTT to your UMH / UNS. MCP for AI agents.

How it works

From ERP push to operator tablet, in three steps.

01

Import your jobs.

Push from your ERP or planning system, drop in a CSV, or use a planning adapter. Routings normalise on the way in.

REST · CSV · Odoo · FrePPLe
02

Operators work the queue.

Each cell sees its own tablet. Tap an operation to start the timer, mark progress, raise issues. The planner sees it live.

Tablet · WebSocket · NCR
03

Planners watch capacity.

A QRM dashboard with WIP limits per cell, due-date heat, and an issue queue. Capacity over 100% gets flagged.

QRM · WIP · Issue queue
